About Our Company


In 2015, CNT Global was established in Shanghai in order to give our customers a quality and sustainable service. Our customers are mostly focused on industrial sectors, such as Automotive,Construction, Tooling Machines,Energy,Transportation,Defence markets with components such as Hydraulic Seals,Pumps,Motors,Valves,Hoses,Fittings.

For your business to improve, smooth and stable supply chain operations is a must. With knowing that, CNT GLOBAL fulfills customer's expectations and demands regarding sourcing goods,products and services globally in order to diversify their procurement operations.

You can select your products from our product range as well as ask for new products searches. Please send us all relevant information,such as product name, code, technical specifications,drawings,photos,samples any other specific information to ensure effective procurement.

Regarding your on-going procurement operations from CHINA, 3rd party/quality inspection service is also given by our technical experts in order our customers to ensure their goods are safe from production to shipment stages.
