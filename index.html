About Our Company


CNT Global was founded in summer of 2015 by a team with the experience of more than 20 years in sealing industry. The aim has been to bring full service to its customers since foundation.


By the virtue of our production technology, we are able to produce high quality seals from any rubber and plastic materials in a very short time. Besides this, with the distributions from domestic and foreign market, we can meet all requirements of sealing industry. In addition to this service, we help our customers with raising of internal productivity by training them in sealing elements. Our company, serving fast and reasonable solutions with its experienced and solution oriented team and not compromising on quality, has a point of advancing itself by giving particular importance to customer satisfaction and maintaining its conformity to ISO 9001 : 2008 Quality Management Systems in all activities.


Our Values


MISSION: To make all the difference in the field of sealing elements by the power of our infrastructure, technology, information and experience.


VISION: To be pioneer foundation by serving high quality product and service with the participation of all employees.
